FormFit — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026 · Developer: Daaymn · Contact: garthwhite507@gmail.com

FormFit ("we", "the app") is a camera-based workout app. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Camera

FormFit uses your device camera to check your exercise form and count reps. All camera processing happens on your device. Camera images and video are never uploaded, stored, or transmitted off your device.

Data stored on your device

Your workout history, programs, and progress are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect or upload this information.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

FormFit shows ads via Google AdMob. To serve ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect a device advertising ID (AD_ID) and limited technical/usage data for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention. This data is processed by Google. See Google's Privacy Policy. You can reset or limit your advertising ID in your device settings.

Data we do NOT collect

We do not collect your name, email, contacts, photos, precise location, or your camera images. FormFit has no account or sign-in.

Children

FormFit is intended for a general audience aged 13 and over.

Security & changes

Ad/analytics traffic is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). We may update this policy; changes will be posted on this page.
