Daaymn Child Safety Policy

Last Updated: July 26, 2024

Our Commitment to Child Safety

Daaymn is committed to creating a safe environment for all our users. We have a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Prohibited Content

  • Images, videos, or audio of child sexual abuse (CSAM).
  • Content that sexually exploits or endangers children.
  • Grooming or soliciting minors for sexual purposes.
  • Content that promotes or enables child trafficking.

Reporting Child Safety Concerns

We provide an in-app reporting feature for suspected CSAE immediately.

Moderation and Enforcement

Our safety team reviews reports and uses automated and manual methods to remove prohibited content.

Cooperation with Law Enforcement

Daaymn cooperates fully with law enforcement agencies in investigating CSAE.
