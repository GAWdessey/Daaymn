How to Delete Your Daaymn Account

To permanently delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Daaymn app on your device.
  2. Navigate to the main "Profile" tab.
  3. Tap the Settings icon (it looks like a gear ⚙️) in the top right corner.
  4. In the Settings menu, scroll to the "Account" section.
  5. Tap on "Delete Account".
  6. Confirm your decision in the pop-up dialog.
Please note: This action is irreversible and will permanently delete your profile, matches, messages, and all other data associated with your account.
