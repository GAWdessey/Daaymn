Privacy Policy for Daaymn

Last Updated: October 30, 2025

Daaymn ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Daaymn app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and why.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Info: name, email, profile info.
  • Geolocation Data: to show nearby users.
  • Usage Data: messages, matches, crash logs.
  • Device Info: device type, OS, IP, push tokens.

2. How We Use Your Info

  • Manage accounts and matches.
  • Enable messaging and notifications.
  • Improve user experience.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We only share data with providers like Supabase, Firebase, and geolocation libraries for functionality.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards. Data is encrypted in transit.

5. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as needed, then delete or anonymize it.

6. Children’s Privacy

Users must be 18+. No data is knowingly collected from children under 18.

7. Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion via email.

8. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy; updates are reflected in "Last Updated".

9. Contact Us

Email: daaymnco@gmail.com
Thank you for using Daaymn. Your privacy matters to us.
