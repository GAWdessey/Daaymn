Privacy Policy for Daaymn

Last Updated: October 30, 2025

Daaymn ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Daaymn mobile app, a social networking and dating service. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and how you can contact us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the core functionality of the app. This includes:

  • Personal Information: name, email address, and user-provided profile information (photos, age, gender, bio).
  • Geolocation Data: proximity location (used only to show nearby users).
  • Usage Data: app activity such as interactions with other users (likes, matches, messages), crash logs, and performance data.
  • Device Information: device type and operating system, IP address, and push notification token (Firebase Cloud Messaging token).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To match users and show nearby profiles based on proximity location.
  • To enable communication between users (messaging, likes, matches).
  • To send push notifications and important updates.
  • To personalize and improve the user experience and to diagnose and fix app issues.

3. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data or use it for advertising. We share data only with third-party service providers that help operate the app. These providers may process data as part of their services:

  • Backend & Database: Supabase
  • Notifications & Analytics: Firebase (Firebase Cloud Messaging)
  • Geolocation Services: Google Play Services
  • Other libraries/packages used by the app (for example: geolocator, google_mlkit_face_detection) may also process data—please review their documentation for details.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. While we strive to protect your data, no internet transmission or storage system is completely secure. Data is encrypted in transit.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. When data is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymize it.

6. Children’s Privacy

Daaymn is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete the information.

7. Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To make such requests, contact us at the email address below. We'll respond within a reasonable timeframe and comply with applicable law.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated within the app.
